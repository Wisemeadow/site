Penny — Privacy Policy

Effective July 6, 2026

Penny is a home-design companion app made by Wise Meadow. It helps households collect inspiration, plan spaces, track budgets, and manage to-dos. This policy explains what information Penny handles and how.

Information we collect

  • Account information. Penny uses Google sign-in. When you sign in we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We never see your Google password.
  • Content you add. Mood-board images, color swatches, notes, photos of your spaces, room dimensions, budgets, and to-do items you create in the app.
  • Connected services (optional). If you choose to connect a third-party service, we store the access credentials that connection requires, encrypted:
    • Asana — used to sync your to-dos with Asana projects you select. Penny reads and writes tasks only in the projects you map.
    • Pinterest — used to import images from your Pinterest boards into your mood boards. Penny only reads the boards and pins you choose to import.
    You can disconnect either service at any time in Penny's settings, which deletes the stored credentials.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your information — to anyone, ever.
  • We do not show ads.
  • We do not use third-party advertising or tracking analytics.

How your information is shared

  • Your household. Content in a house is visible to the people who are members of that house, according to the role they were given (owner, editor, or viewer).
  • Share links. You can create a read-only link to a mood board to show someone outside your household. Anyone with the link can view that one board (and nothing else) until the link expires or you revoke it.
  • Connected services. If you link a space to an Asana project, the to-dos in that space sync to that project. Nothing is sent to Pinterest; imports are read-only.

Where your information lives

Penny runs on Google Cloud (application hosting and image storage) and Supabase (database and authentication). Data is encrypted in transit, and credentials for connected services are additionally encrypted at rest. These providers process data on our behalf and do not use it for their own purposes.

Data retention and deletion

Your content stays in Penny until you delete it or ask us to. Deleting an item, space, or photo removes it from the app. Disconnecting Asana or Pinterest deletes the stored credentials for that service. To delete your account and all associated data, email us and we'll take care of it promptly.

Children

Penny is a household planning tool intended for adults. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we'll update this page and the effective date above. Meaningful changes will be called out to signed-in users.
