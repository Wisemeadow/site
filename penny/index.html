Penny

A home-design companion for your household.

What Penny does

  • Mood boards. A freeform canvas per room — drag and arrange inspiration images, color swatches, and sticky notes until the space feels right.
  • A house style guide. Your palette and the look you're going for, in one place, so every room's plans stay on-brand for your home.
  • Room planning. Photos of the real space, dimensions, and notes alongside the inspiration.
  • Budgets. Estimate vs. actual per line item, per room, rolled up across the whole house.
  • To-dos. A simple project list per room — optionally synced two-way with the Asana projects you choose.
  • Pinterest import. Bring pins from your own Pinterest boards straight onto your mood boards.
  • Your household, your rules. Invite family members as owners, editors, or viewers — and share a board read-only with anyone via an expiring link.
