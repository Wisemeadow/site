Penny

Penny is our home-design companion for households: collect inspiration on freeform mood boards, plan each room with photos and dimensions, keep budgets honest, and manage project to-dos — with optional connections to the tools you already use, like Asana and Pinterest.

Penny is currently in private use with our founding households, with more on the way.
